Hello, I am

I’m an Italian guy living near the Apennine Mountains. You’ll usually find me searching for the steepest roads to tackle on my road bike or exploring new trails to hike on the weekends.

I do my best to blend a love for nature with the technology we live with today. Thanks to my strong sense of direction (north is always set in my head!) and passion for maps, you can count on me to never get us lost on a hike.
